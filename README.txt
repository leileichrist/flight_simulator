A very simple flight simulator program.

--make to compile

Press  'Up' arrow key to pitch up.
Press  'Down' arrow key to pitch down.
Press  'Left' arrow key to roll left.
Press  'Right' arrow key to roll right.

Press  'W' to increase the flying speed
Press  'S' to slow down the plane.
Press  'A' to increase the rolling angle increment
Press  'D' to decrease the rolling angle increment


Note: the plane will be flying automatically. 

Press 'Esc' to quit.
